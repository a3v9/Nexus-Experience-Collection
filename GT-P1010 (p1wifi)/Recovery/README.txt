------- WARNING --------
This is the files needed for the recovery, which is needed for the P1010 to flash Nexus Experience.

After flashing the recovery, if your device is rendered unusable, we are NOT in any way responsible.
Make sure to follow every single step of flashing correctly.

------ INSTRUCTIONS -----
1. Boot your device into Download Mode by pressing Volume Down + Power Button.
2. Connect your device into your computer, and make sure to have the Samsung USB Drivers installed.
3. Download the recovery file.
4. Open Odin v1.61, make sure device is connected, and in PDA slot, select the downloaded recovery image. 
Do not use a PIT file neither check the repartition box.
5. Let the process run.
6. Turn off your tablet, then once it is turned off, hold Power + Volume Up to enter CWM.
